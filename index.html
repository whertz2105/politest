What Politeion is

A civic instrument for reading politics past the left–right line. Three parts, one 22-axis model:

  • The compass — a self-test that places you on 22 independent axes (economy, culture, liberty, state power, foreign policy and more), then matches you to ideological archetypes and historical figures.
  • The Analyzer — paste any article and see the stance of the piece scored on those same axes, with a verbatim quote behind every score.
  • The Daily Brief — a neutral daily read whose every item is machine-checked for no detectable stance before it's published.

No accounts required. Your results are anonymous.
